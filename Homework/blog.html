Classification of sports yachts

by Arslkv / January 25, 2019 / 343

Sailing competitions are usually held in yachts of the same design and consist of one or a series of races, which together are called regatta. For each race, each yacht takes a place, and this yacht is assigned the number of points according to its place in the race. The winner of the regatta is the one who gains the best number of points according to the results of the races that make up the regatta.

However, the yachts in each class can differ from each other and can be adjusted differently. On yachts, you can change the inclination and bend of the mast, the aerodynamic shape of the sail, the angle of the sail to the yacht and the wind, the yacht's tilt and a number of other parameters. The material and cutting of the sails may also vary. The speed of the yacht is very much dependent on the settings, control techniques and qualities of the crew. Each crew adjusts its yacht for its weight, height and manner of management, as well as for specific wave conditions, wind power and the course of the yacht. For large yachts, it is not always possible to build all yachts strictly according to the same rules, and then each yacht is measured according to a set of parameters and then special coefficients are calculated using a special formula with which the yacht’s distance is recalculated and then the calculated times for all participating yachts are compared . Such competitions are called "handicap races."

Yachting

by Arslkv / January 25, 2019 / 343

In addition to Sailing itself, there is an even broader concept - Yachting, which can be described as sailing tourism, since in most cases it no longer carries directly a competitive component, but rather refers to leisure.
Yachting involves a wide range of actions: choosing a ship (owning or renting it), determining the yacht direction and the itinerary, leisure activities associated with the yacht (swimming in the sea, diving, water skiing, fishing, etc.), but desirable optional command of a ship. For tourism purposes, you can engage in yachting as a crew member or lessee of the vessel.

There is a large list of yacht destinations, each of which is characterized by its own nuances, for example, climatic features, attractions, itineraries, number of marinas, etc.
Yachting is possible on all types of yachts: sailing, motor, gulets, sail-motor catamarans.
